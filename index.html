Purpose

Mail and package theft is a hot topic of news outlets nowadays. Every year, the United States Postal Service investigates about 4,000 reports of suspicious substance and items in the mail, including mail bombs. Amazon attempted to address this problem with in home delivery, but was met with lash back by consumers unwilling to give strangers access to their homes. Given this, we believe mailbox security is the solution.

Mailbox owners deserve to know exactly who is using their mailbox and when they are doing so. Our project looks to solve this very real problem, along with a few added features to improve user experience.
